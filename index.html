Our Story

About RCCG Global Drama

A ministry born from a divine mandate — using the timeless art of drama to spread the eternal gospel of Jesus Christ to every nation on earth.

✦
🎯
Our Mission
To glorify God through the creative arts — specifically drama — by communicating the transformative power of the gospel in ways that cut through language, culture, and background. We are committed to raising spirit-filled performers who carry the anointing of God onto every stage, street, and screen.
🌍
Our Vision
To be the premier Christian drama ministry in the world — establishing drama departments in every RCCG parish globally, and producing world-class productions that win souls, disciple believers, and transform entire communities through the power of God's word performed with excellence.
Our History

A Journey of Faith

2008
Founded at RCCG National Headquarters
RCCG Global Drama was established by a small group of passionate believers who felt a divine call to use drama as a tool for evangelism at the Redemption Camp, Lagos.
2011
First International Drama Tour
The ministry conducted its first overseas outreach to the United Kingdom, performing to packed venues and seeing over 500 first-time decisions for Christ.
2015
Launch of the Drama Training School
Recognizing the need for excellence in ministry, we established a formal training programme to equip drama ministers with professional-grade skills rooted in godly character.
2018
Global Expansion to 30+ Nations
Drama departments were formally established in RCCG parishes across Africa, Europe, North America, and Asia, bringing the total ministry footprint to over 30 countries.
2024
Digital Ministry Launch
RCCG Global Drama launched its digital platform — streaming productions to millions worldwide and opening the doors for online membership, training, and global community.
